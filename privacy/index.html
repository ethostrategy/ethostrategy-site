Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Who we are

Ethostrategy is a consulting practice founded and operated by Madhuri Gujje. Our website is ethostrategy.co.

Information we collect

We collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, organization name (optional), and message. These are processed through Netlify Forms and delivered to us via email.
  • Email correspondence — if you email us directly at ethostrategy@gmail.com, we retain the contents of that exchange.

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts on this website.

How we use your information

Information you submit through our contact form or via email is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Evaluate whether our services are a good fit for your organization
  • Schedule and conduct introductory conversations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

This website is hosted on Netlify. Contact form submissions are processed and stored by Netlify Forms. Netlify's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy.

Fonts are served by Fontshare (by Indian Type Foundry). No personal data is shared with Fontshare beyond standard HTTP request headers.

Data retention

We retain contact form submissions and email correspondence for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and evaluate potential engagements. If you would like your data deleted, contact us and we will remove it promptly.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you

To exercise any of these rights, email ethostrategy@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out at ethostrategy@gmail.com.
