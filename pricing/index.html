A note on sliding scale

Our pricing is sliding-scale on purpose. Organizations with real budgets pay published rates. Organizations with less pay what they can, and organizations with nothing often get pro-bono capacity when we have it. We do this because the communities we care about can't afford to only be served by organizations that can afford us. If you'd like to talk about fit, get in touch.
