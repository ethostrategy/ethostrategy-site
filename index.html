The Framework

Five pillars that hold every decision up to the light.

Every strategy is anchored in our five pillars and developed alongside those closest to the issue.

Consent

Is participation truly voluntary, fully informed, and free from pressure?

Representation

Are the people closest to the issue driving the decisions?

Dignity

Is dignity honored before, during, and after the work?

Sovereignty

Do communities keep control over their own futures?

Accountability

Are commitments measurable, public, and followed through?

Beyond Ethics

We also look at how your organization runs.

Ethics is the bar. Organizational health is the blueprint.

Strategy

Do you know what you're doing, why, and how?

People

Is your team well-governed, well-treated, and built to last?

Systems

Do your systems support secure, efficient operations?

Finance

Are you financially stable, and independently reviewed?

Measurement

Are you quantifying your impact and growing from it?
