Dignified Menstruation.

A global advocacy organization working to end period stigma and expand access. A community-led research framework and an advocacy strategy rooted in sovereignty and representation.

DM

The engagement

Dignified Menstruation works across multiple regions with very different cultural contexts. The risk in any global advocacy effort: a single narrative that flattens local experience and accidentally imposes outside framings on local advocates. We're working with them on a community-led research framework where local advocates own their narratives, data, and advocacy priorities — and a representation policy that pushes lived-experience voices into strategic decision roles, not photo ops.

Status: in progress

This engagement is active. A full case study — with framework details, outcomes, and reflections — will be published here once the work concludes and we have the consent of the organization and the communities involved to share it publicly.
